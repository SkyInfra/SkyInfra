<details>
<summary><b>🔧 Self-Hosting Fallback (click to expand)</b></summary>
<br>

The Stats, Streak, and Trophy cards above pull from **free public APIs** (`github-readme-stats.vercel.app`, `streak-stats.demolab.com`, `github-profile-trophy.vercel.app`). These occasionally go down due to shared rate limits. If cards stay broken for more than a day, self-host your own copy in under 5 minutes:

1. **Stats & Top Langs:** Fork [github-readme-stats](https://github.com/anuraghazra/github-readme-stats) → deploy free on Vercel → replace the domain in the image URLs above with your own deployment.
2. **Streak Stats:** Fork [github-readme-streak-stats](https://github.com/DenverCoder1/github-readme-streak-stats) → deploy → swap the URL.
3. **Trophies:** Fork [github-profile-trophy](https://github.com/ryo-ma/github-profile-trophy) → deploy → swap the URL.

This removes any dependency on shared rate limits and guarantees your cards always render.

</details>
